About me

I'm a Senior Network Engineer in Chicago with 8+ years of experience managing enterprise-scale networks across 12 hospitals, 3 datacenters, and 100+ clinics. My expertise spans Cisco Catalyst/Nexus, Palo Alto NGFW, Aruba wireless, and automation with Python and Ansible.
